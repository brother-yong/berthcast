How the AI uses these profiles

When the AI analyses your inventory, it looks up each item's supplier here. A high delay rate (above 30%) triggers a high-risk flag — the AI will require a mitigation plan before recommending an order. If a supplier isn't listed, confidence is automatically Insufficient data and the AI says so rather than guessing. Lead time determines how far ahead the AI looks when deciding if stock is running low.
