Step 1

What files to prepare

BerthAI works with exports from your ERP. No reformatting required — export them as Excel (.xlsx) or CSV. You can upload up to four file types. Two are required; the other two make a large difference to accuracy.

File Required? What it's used for Without it
Inventory report Required Current stock levels and item list. This is the foundation of every analysis. Analysis cannot run.
Sales report Required Sales history per item. Used to calculate how fast each item moves and how long stock will last. Analysis cannot run. Without velocity data, order quantities show as "Verify with team" and confidence shows as N/A.
Purchase orders Recommended Links each item to its supplier. Without this, BerthAI cannot tell you which supplier to contact. All items show "Unknown supplier". Confidence drops to N/A on every recommendation.
Supplier list Recommended Tells BerthAI whether each supplier is import (long lead time) or local (short lead time). Directly affects stock runway calculations. All suppliers treated as "other" with unknown lead times. Runway estimates will be unreliable.
Step 2

Column requirements

BerthAI detects columns automatically. As long as your column names are close to the ones listed below, the system will find them. The exact names in your ERP export are usually fine — you do not need to rename anything.

Inventory report Required
What BerthAI needs Column names it recognises Required? Effect if missing
Item name description, item_description, inventory_desc, product_description, item_name, product_name Required Analysis fails — cannot identify items.
Stock quantity qty_on_hand, qty, quantity, stock_on_hand, closing_stock, balance, stock_balance Required Analysis fails — cannot assess stock levels.
Category / storage type category, cat, class, item_category, product_category, storage_type Recommended Cannot determine chill / frozen / dry. Spoilage risk is less accurate.
Unit of measure uom, unit_of_measure, unit, uom_code, base_uom, purchase_uom Recommended Order quantities show as "units" instead of kg / cartons / pcs.
Sales report Required
What BerthAI needs Column names it recognises Required? Effect if missing
Item name Same as inventory — see above Required Cannot link sales data to inventory items.
Date date — must be exactly this Required System cannot count months of data. Velocity calculations may be off.
Quantity sold billing_qty, qty, quantity, billing_quantity Required Cannot calculate average monthly sales or suggested order quantities.
Revenue / amount net_amount, total_amount, billing_amount, sales_value, revenue, ext_price Recommended Cannot rank items by revenue when analysing top SKUs only.
Purchase orders Recommended
What BerthAI needs Column names it recognises Required? Effect if missing
Item name Same as inventory — see above Required Cannot link items to suppliers.
Supplier name Any column containing "supplier" and "name", or just "supplier" Required All items show "Unknown supplier."
Supplier list Recommended
What BerthAI needs Column names it recognises Required? Effect if missing
Supplier name Any column containing "name" or "supplier" Required Cannot match suppliers to lead times.
Supplier type Any column containing "type", "category", or "class" — value must include the word import or local Required All suppliers treated as unknown. Lead times will be wrong.

How lead times work. Suppliers classified as import are given a 16-week lead time. Suppliers classified as local are given a 3-week lead time. If a supplier's type is not recognised, lead time is marked as unknown and the recommendation confidence drops.

Step 3

The upload process

Once your files are ready, the process takes under 10 minutes from start to purchase order.

1
Upload your files

Go to New Analysis and upload each file into its matching slot. You can upload Excel (.xlsx) or CSV. Files must be under 100 MB each.

2
Review duplicate item names

BerthAI scans item names across all your files and flags names that refer to the same product but are written differently — e.g. "Bottled Water 1.5L" vs "BTL WATER 1.5L". Confirm the groupings so sales data links correctly to inventory.

3
Add any context the AI should know

Before the analysis runs, you can tell BerthAI about things that affect purchasing decisions — delayed suppliers, a large upcoming order, items you're discontinuing. This is optional but improves the recommendations.

4
Wait for the analysis

Three AI agents run in parallel — one for inventory health, one for reorder quantities, and one for risk assessment. This usually takes 2–5 minutes depending on the size of your data.

5
Review and approve recommendations

Each recommendation shows the item, suggested order quantity, supplier, stock runway, and a plain-English reason. Approve the ones you want to action, add notes if needed, then print or download your purchase order.

Good to know

Tips for best results

Use at least 6 months of sales data

The system calculates monthly averages from your sales history. Less than 3 months of data can make fast-moving items look slower than they are.

The date column must be named "date"

This is the one strict naming requirement. If your sales file has a date column with a different name (e.g. "invoice_date" or "txn_date"), rename it to date before uploading.

Item names must be consistent across files

If inventory says "White Rice 5kg" and sales says "RICE WHITE 5KG", BerthAI's deduplication step will try to match them — but the closer the names, the more reliable the match.

Supplier type must say "import" or "local"

The system does a simple text match. "Import supplier", "Local vendor", and "Imported goods" all work. Words like "overseas" or "domestic" will not be recognised.

Upload the most recent PO export

BerthAI uses the most recent purchase order per item to identify the current supplier. An old PO file may map items to suppliers you no longer use.

N/A confidence means supplier is unknown

If a recommendation shows N/A confidence, it means BerthAI could not find the item's supplier in your purchase orders file. Upload a PO file to resolve this.
