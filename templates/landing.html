Why this is expensive

A stockout doesn't warn you. It just arrives.

A high-runner quietly draws down. A supplier slips a week. The first real signal is a customer order you can't fill — and on import SKUs with lead times measured in months, that gap isn't a bad afternoon. It's weeks of orders walking out the door.

The math is unforgiving on the items that matter most. The faster a SKU sells, the less warning you get, and the longer the lead time, the earlier you needed to act. Spreadsheets don't flag that. berthcast does — for every line, every run.

What berthcast does

Three agents work your data, step by step

Upload your exports once. No setup, no configuration, no spreadsheet formulas to maintain.

Inventory health analysis

Classifies every SKU as CRITICAL, LOW, or NORMAL from days of stock left versus your real lead times — import versus local.

Reorder recommendations

Exact reorder quantity per item, accounting for sales velocity, spoilage thresholds, and supplier lead times. No guesswork.

Stockout risk flagging

Flags which items are most likely to stock out based on current velocity, on-hand levels, and lead times — so you act before the gap, not after.

Supplier-aware planning

Knows which suppliers are import versus local and applies the right buffer automatically, so orders land before you run dry.

Printable purchase orders

Approve recommendations with one click. berthcast generates a formatted PO you can send directly — no reformatting.

Chat with your data

Ask in plain language. "Which items are critical this week?" or "What do I order before the long weekend?" Answered instantly.
