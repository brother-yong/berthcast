Common questions

What files do I need to use berthcast?

An inventory file and a sales report are the minimum. Purchase orders and supplier data improve accuracy significantly. See the user guide for the exact column requirements.

What does the free analysis actually show me?

A full inventory health report — every SKU flagged by status (Critical, Low, OK, Overstocked), days of supply remaining, and specific purchase recommendations with quantities. The same output as paid plans, just capped at one run.

How does billing work for Professional and Enterprise?

We invoice monthly via bank transfer or PayNow. There's no automated payment system yet — contact us and we'll set it up directly. No lock-in, cancel any time with 30 days notice.

Is my data secure?

Your uploaded files are stored on a private server and are only used to run your analysis. They're never shared, sold, or used to train AI models. See our data page for exactly how we handle it.
