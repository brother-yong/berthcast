What stockouts really cost

A stockout doesn't warn you. It just arrives.

A high-runner quietly draws down. A supplier slips a week. The first real signal is a customer order you can't fill — and on import lines with lead times measured in months, that gap isn't a bad afternoon. It's weeks of orders walking out the door.

The maths is unforgiving on the items that matter most. The faster a line sells, the less warning you get; the longer the lead time, the earlier you needed to act. Spreadsheets don't flag that. berthcast does — every line, every run.
