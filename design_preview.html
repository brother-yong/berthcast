Note about the dark navy tile

The new logo is transparent — clean alpha channel, no white box. That solves placement on coloured surfaces. One small limitation: the ship in your logo is the same deep navy as my Navy 800 surface, so on a dark navy background the hull blends into the surface and only the gold chart stays visible. The fix is simple — keep the nav bar light. I've switched the in-app nav from navy to white in the mockup below, which makes the whole ship read cleanly.
