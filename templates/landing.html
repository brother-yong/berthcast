What BerthAI does

Three AI agents working in parallel on your data

Upload your exports once. BerthAI does the rest — no setup, no configuration, no spreadsheet formulas.

Inventory health analysis
Classifies every SKU as CRITICAL, LOW, or NORMAL based on days of stock remaining vs your actual lead times — import vs local suppliers.
Reorder recommendations
Calculates exact reorder quantities per item, accounting for sales velocity, spoilage thresholds, and supplier lead times. No guesswork.
Stockout pattern detection
Cross-references your stockout history against current stock levels to flag items most likely to go out again in the next 30 days.
Supplier-aware planning
Knows which suppliers are import (16-week lead) vs local (3-week). Applies the right buffer automatically so orders land before you run dry.
Printable purchase orders
Approve recommendations with one click. BerthAI generates a formatted purchase order you can send directly — no reformatting needed.
Chat with your data
Ask questions in plain language. "Which items are critical this week?" or "What should I order before the long weekend?" BerthAI answers instantly.
